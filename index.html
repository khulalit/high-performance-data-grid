High performance data grid

Dive into the world of handling massive datasets this data grid! Built to smoothly render up to a million rows (or maybe more not limit, not be honest just how you store is the issue), it offers smooth scrolling and razor-sharp responsiveness that keeps you in the flow. The secret? Intelligent virtualization that renders only the cells in view with virtualized scrolling, paired with very optimized and granular DOM updates. scrolling and a slick custom scrollbar for effortless navigation. Here, I am here only focused on taming the challenge of large-scale rendering. Storing and handling data is saved for the sequel. And this logic scales horizontally too, perfect for those epic wide datasets!
